## How I Solve Problems

I solve problems by breaking them into smaller parts, looking for symptoms, tracing where the issue starts, and testing fixes step by step.

My usual problem-solving questions are:

- What is supposed to happen?
- What is actually happening?
- What changed?
- What parts are connected?
- Where is the flow breaking?
- What control is missing?
- What evidence supports the issue?
- What fix would restore stability?
